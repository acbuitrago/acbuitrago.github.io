ICDOT

Previous Next

Developed the newest version of the iOS and Android app. This app assist truckers in spotting DOT Inspection Stations while they are traveling the nation's highways. A user can generate a geotagged tip indicating that he found an inspection of the Department of Transportation or other eventuality; later, the system generates a push notification for the users that are in a radio near the tip.

Additionally, the application performs voice recognition to generate tips, allowing truckers not to stop to type the tip. On the other hand, the application uses AdMob to generate advertising within the application.
